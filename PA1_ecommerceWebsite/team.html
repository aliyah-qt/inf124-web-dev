Who Are We

We’re more than just a drink station—we’re a uniquely handcrafted experience. When you step through our doors, you’ll be welcomed by smiling employees and an upbeat atmosphere. Our extensive menu is filled with lots of handcrafted drinks, so don’t be afraid to ask the cashier any questions you may have! We’re always happy to go over options with you to adapt to any of your dietary requests. While you’re here, feel free to connect to our free WiFi or check out our board game station while you enjoy your beverage. And if you have work to do, take advantage of our free printing.


Kermit the Frog

Back in 1969, Kermit had a dream of owning his own boba store. And after the acquire of a plot of land in 1978 he was able to start building what is now known as the Unoriginal Boba Store.

Over the years Kermit learned to craft such delicious drinks that has won him The Bay Area's best Boba Stores title.

in 2012, Kermit stepped down as CEO and has since contributed his earings to charities and other humanitarian efforts

Big Bird

Big Bird was college friends with Kermit and when his best buddy had the idea to go big with his dream, he pitched in.

Mr. Bird became the Co-Founder of The Unoriginal Boba Store and was the prime taste tester in his buddy Kermit's kitchen concoctions.

Bert

Meet Bert our cherished Media Outreach chair! Although he is newer to the team his efforts to the cause have been wildly

incomparable. Bert played a huge role in organzing major partnerships and promoting this brand through various platforms.
